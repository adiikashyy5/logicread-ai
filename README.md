# LogicRead AI

**AI-powered reading comprehension tool** — paste any text, ask questions, and get precise answers with evidence highlighted directly from the source.

🔗 **Live Demo:** [logic-reading-app-final.vercel.app](https://logic-reading-app-final.vercel.app)

---

## What it does

LogicRead AI lets you interrogate any piece of text using natural language. Instead of skimming through long passages manually, you paste the content, ask a question, and the AI finds the exact answer — pointing back to the evidence in the original text.

Built for students, researchers, and anyone who needs to extract meaning from dense reading material fast.

---

## Features

- 📄 Paste any text — articles, essays, textbook passages, reports
- 💬 Ask questions in plain English
- 🔍 Get answers with evidence retrieved directly from the source
- ⚡ Instant responses powered by AI
- 🌐 Fully deployed and accessible via browser — no setup needed

## Project background

LogicRead AI was independently built and deployed as a demonstration of applied AI in the EdTech space. The tool targets a real gap — students and test-takers who need to process large volumes of reading material quickly and accurately.

It was also developed as part of a university course project targeting NSW Selective High School test preparation, with a competitor analysis and market research component alongside the working prototype.
